About US

Hello!!

This is an adult Care Homes which is licensed, and offer single family residences that offer 24-hour care for up to four adult residents in a homelike setting ran by Operator Manju Thapa Magar. This home is located in residential neighborhoods. This Care Home offers individualized care while supporting and honoring a resident’s independence, cultural needs, preferences, and choices.

Operators and caregivers participate in on-going care programs and training. This home is licensed annually and monitored quarterly via unannounced home visits by Multnomah County’s Aging, Disability and Veterans Services’ Adult Care Home Program.

--- What services do residents receive in this Adult Care Home?
  • Assistance with daily activities like bathing, dressing, walking, eating, and using the restroom
  • Medication management, meal,
  • Housekeeping and laundry service,
  • Arrangement of transportation to appointments and other activities.
Who lives in an Adult Care Home?
  • Older Adults and People with Disabilities (APD).
  • Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities (I/DD)
  • Mental Health and Addictions (MHA)
